FAQ's

Where do Meteoride Operate ?
Meteoride offer bike rentals all over in states listed on our home page.
Is Delivery and Collection Included ?
Delivery and collection is generally included in our rental fees, when stated, but charges may vary based on quantity of bikes and delivery or collection location.
How do I Rent a Bicycle ?
Simply give us your dates, bike preference and departure. - we'll handle the rest.
Can Meteoride collect bicycle from a different location to delivery ?
Absolutely! It's no problem to have a bicycle delivered in one location and collected from a different location. For example we could deliver bikes in Mumbai-Marine Drive and collect them from Mumbai-Carter Road.
Can bicycle also be bought if liked?
Yes, you can also buy a bicycle. We have brand new bicycle to be sold of same quality as in rentals.
Do you offer Road Bicycle Hire ?
Yes, we offer Road Bicycle here.
Is there any penalty for late return?
Yes,but if you are late by more than 30mins and for those we take extra charges.
What is the cancellation policy?
The deposit will always be refunded back irrespective of the time.
If there is a cancellation, then the following policy will be applied
No show - 100% deduction and only security deposit will be refunded.
Between 0 - 6 hrs of the pick up time - 75% rental charges will be withheld and security deposit will be refunded.
Between 6-24 hrs of the pick up time: 50% rental charges will be withheld.
Between 24-72 hrs of the pick up time: 25% rental charges will be withheld.
72 hrs or more prior to the pick up time: 10% rental charges will be withheld.
If we cancel the booking from our side due to any booking issues, technical error, non-availability of the vehicle,
we will return the entire rental amount and the deposit.
